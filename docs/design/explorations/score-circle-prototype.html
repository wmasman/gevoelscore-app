Score-circle gesture prototype

Tap "Open sheet" below. Drag horizontally on the circle.

  1. Drag right: number goes up. Drag left: number goes down.
  2. Snap to integer on release. Per integer crossed: scale-pulse.
  3. Try to drag the circle slightly down-and-right. The sheet must not dismiss.
  4. Drag the small handle bar at the top of the sheet downwards. The sheet should dismiss past ~100px.
  5. Tap the dimmed backdrop: sheet dismisses immediately.

This represents the day-overview behind the sheet. Would normally show the today-card with score, note, and tags.

A second card to give the sheet something to dim against.
