However, within the headline cell the verdict bar is still a 3-condition gate with three independent p-value tests at α = 0.05 each, and the **PARTIAL band (2-of-3 MET) has no multiplicity correction across the three p-values**. The effective false-PARTIAL rate is higher than 0.7% but neither explicitly bounded nor reported. Magnitude: **minor** (the same minor that fired on the HA-C3 v1 review and was partially absorbed into the v2 §3.8 gate template). PARTIAL is descriptively informative and §9.2 explicitly disclaims it from SUPPORTED-bar weight, so this does not leak into the headline verdict; one sentence in §5.1 closes it.
